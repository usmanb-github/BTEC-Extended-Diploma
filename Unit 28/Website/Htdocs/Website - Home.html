Arsenal want to tie up €3M for Bielik [Deal Completed]

Arsenal are confident of completing the purchase Legia Warsaw's midfield prodigy after going back to the Polish club with an increased bid of €3million (£2.4million). Arsene has shown a clear interest for the young midfielder, but he will be viewed as a long-term prospect rather than this season. Arsene is keen in Krystian Bielik's versatility. He can play as an holding-midfielder and as a center back, two areas Arsene is keen to strengthen. Wenger has recently said: “He is a young boy that we follow, yes. We are not very close at the moment [to agree a deal].” The midfielder has claimed that he wants to join Arsenal.

Chelsea target Jese and Varane

Despite Jose Mourinho outlining he has no plans to add to his squad, according to Daily Express; Chelsea are looking to bolster their team by "RAIDING" Real Madrid's defender Raphael Varane and foward Jese Rodriquez. Already, I think he's the best." Jose Mourinho is a fan of both of them, and now he wants them. Would they both leave?

Steven Gerrard leaving Liverpool

Heading over to Liverpool's news, Steven Gerrard has announced that he will be leaving Liverpool at the end of the season. Many speculations are that Gerrard will be playing in the USA for LA Galaxy. Former teammates have paid tribute to him. Gary Lineker also tweeted, "Seems Steven Gerrard is set to leave Liverpool at the end of the season. One of the finest players to have graced the PL. A top, top player."
